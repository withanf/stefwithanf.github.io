Professionally

I'm a manager of product design at the EY Design Studio. I'm a multidisciplinary designer with a background in data analysis and psychology, a passion for visual design, and a UX certification from Nielsen Norman Group.

I have over 7 years of experience producing award-winning designs for Fortune 100, public sector, and startup clients. My work spans the full define-to-development lifecycle, including research, strategy, prototyping, design systems, partnering with developers to bring design into production, and user testing and analytics. Additionally, I have experience improving cross-functional design processes and building design teams.

Mental health and IDEA (inclusivity, diversity, equity, and accessibility) are passions of mine. Nobody is an "edge case", and my work reflects that.
